The Team

Zach Preston

Zach is a senior majoring in computer science at the University of Idaho.

Zach is the team's Glorius Leader and is responsible for coordinating with team mentors, designing the project's architecture, and writing software for the project's robot, Fred.

Austin Kugler

Austin is a senior majoring in computer science at the University of Idaho. His interests include backend, fintech, edtech, and distributed systems. Most recently, he worked as a software development engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the Amazon Connect organization. Austin's work experience covers system design, fullstack development, decentralized applications, data visualization, distributed systems, and higher education.

On the Keynetics team, Austin is responsible for fullstack development of the KeyAssembler web app. He also works with team members to design and implement an architecture for dispensing and curing resin using a 6-axis robot.

Taylor Martin

Taylor is a senior majoring in computer science at the University of Idaho.

John Myers

Fred

Fred is a 6-axis myCobot 320-Pi robot. He is responsible for dispensing, curing resin, and looking cool.
